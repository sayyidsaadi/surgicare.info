About us

The Surgicare hospital located in Cumilla offers modern medical services with the aid of 3D-4K technology. It is a unique facility for diagnosing diseases, complex surgical services, gynecological and fertility services with the help of laparoscopic procedures. Since 2010, our successful surgeries of all types have been made possible through the use of the latest 'Bipolar Ligasure Vessel' sealer from the German STORZ brand. We have a proud track record of successful surgeries in your town. We are committed in modern and quality healthcare services.
